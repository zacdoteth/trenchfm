# Podium (trench.fm) — Elevator Pitch & Suno Brief

## The One-Liner
**Podium is a live 3D arena where crypto degens gather to hear callers pitch tokens, vote on theses, and trade with one tap — appointment TV meets Wall Street meets the metaverse.**

## Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)

Imagine a neon-lit cyberpunk colosseum. 400 avatars packed into a glowing room, staring up at a jumbotron. A caller steps onto the podium and pitches their token — live charts, live data, live conviction. The crowd votes bullish or bearish. You can buy instantly, right there, one tap. The caller earns when you win. The room erupts. That's Podium.

We're turning crypto trading from a lonely scroll through Twitter into an **appointment event** — 1-3 featured calls per day, artificial scarcity, concentrated attention. Think Clubhouse meets Robinhood inside a Refik Anadol art installation.

## The Vibe (for Suno)

- **Setting:** Neon-drenched 3D arena, hot pink and electric cyan, flowing organic shaders, pulsing lights
- **Energy:** Hype, FOMO, adrenaline, conviction, community — a crowd going wild when the chart pumps
- **Culture:** Crypto degen, "the trenches", diamond hands, ape in, WAGMI, LFG
- **Sound palette:** Cyberpunk bass, synth waves, crowd energy, arcade/retro undertones (Press Start 2P font energy), future bass drops
- **Mood:** You're in the arena. The lights are on you. The crowd is watching. Make your call.

## Key Themes for Lyrics

1. **The Podium** — stepping up, making the call, all eyes on you
2. **The Trenches** — where the real ones trade, where alpha lives
3. **Neon lights** — hot pink, electric cyan, flowing colors, the glow
4. **The crowd** — 400 deep, voting, watching, vibing together
5. **One tap** — instant conviction, buy the thesis, send it
6. **Appointment with destiny** — the daily call, the moment everyone tunes in
7. **FOMO & fire** — if you're not in the room, you're missing it

A theme you can loop forever — hypnotic, groovy, warm but energetic. Think: late night in a glowing arcade, controllers in hand, bass rattling the floor.
